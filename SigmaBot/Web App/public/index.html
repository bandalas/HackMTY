Cooked and Dry Meats

Sigma produces and sells cooked and dry hams and sausages, bologna, and bacon among other products. Our brands are widely known among consumers, since they are focused on satisfying the needs in each segment of the selected market. Some of them have more than 70 years of tradition.

Lasagna covered with Ham, Cheese and Spinach

Ingredients:

8 sheets of lasagna
8 slices of ham
2 ½ cups of cottage cheese
1 package fresh spinach
1 cup shredded gouda cheese
Water and salt to boil the pasta
Salt and Pepper to taste
Tomato sauce

Preparation:

Set the oven in 375ªF. Boil the fresh spinach leaves with a little water and salt until they reduce. Take them out of the water and dry with a towel. Once they are dry, cut into small pieces, mix very well with cottage cheese and add salt and pepper to taste. Cover the bottom of pan with tomato sauce. Cut the ham in slices similar to the lasagna sheets. Spread a bit of the cottage cheese and spinach on the pasta and top with a slice of ham. Carefully roll the pasta until if forms a small roll; place on mold and cover with more tomato sauce. Spread with shredded gouda cheese and cover with aluminum paper. Place in previously heated oven during 30 minutes, remove the aluminum paper and bake for 15 more minutes so the cheese melts and the pasta browns.

Sliced Peppers with Cheese

Ingredients:

250 g panela cheese
1/2 stick margarine
200 g cream
5 poblano peppers grilled
1 finely chopped onion
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon oregano
5 epazote leaves finely chopped
4 cups tomato sauce seasoned

Preparation:

Peel the poblano peppers, remove seeds and cut into slices. Fry the onions with the margarine, when they are soft and clear add the pepper slices, salt, oregano and epazote: let cook for three minutes. Add tomato sauce and let boil for five minutes. Serve pepper slices and spread with panela cheese as well as cream. Serve with refried beans and hot tortillas

Fiesta Dip

Ingredients:

250 g double cream cheese
200 g cream
2 tablespoons finely chopped onions
2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh epazote
1/2 teaspoon salt

Preparation:

Whip the double cream cheese and cream until it has a homogeneous mix. Incorporate the finely chopped onion and epazote. Pour in a container and place in the center of a dish, served with toasted tortillas for tasting.

Sigma acquires the Peruvian packaged meats company SUPEMSA

June 28, 2017

San Pedro Garza Garcia, N.L., Mexico, June 28, 2017.- Sigma Alimentos, S.A. Of C.V. ("Sigma"), a company dedicated to the production, marketing and distribution of refrigerated food, announced today that it has acquired Sociedad Suizo Peruana de Embutidos, S.A. (“SUPEMSA”) a Peruvian company engaged in the production and commercialization of packaged meats and dairy products.

SUPEMSA is leader in the production of high quality packaged meats with more than 25 years of success in its field, through recognized local brands such as Otto Kunz and La Segoviana, with which it serves different market segments. The company operates a packaged meats plant in the city of Chancay where it employs more than 800 collaborators. In 2016, it generated sales of $ 54.2 million USD.

This acquisition is aligned with Sigma's growth strategy by consolidating its position in Peru in the categories in which it currently participates.
